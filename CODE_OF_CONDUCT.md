# Code of Conduct

Bardo should be a welcoming place for people building, playing, testing, and learning together.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction when impact does not match intent.
- Keep criticism focused on ideas, code, docs, and project direction.
- Help newcomers understand the project without making them feel foolish.
- Respect privacy, safety, and boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized, violent, or demeaning comments directed at another person.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, discussions, reviews, or community spaces.
- Bad-faith behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or exhausting.

## Reporting

If you see or experience unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub. Do not create public callout issues for sensitive reports.

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, block users, or take other moderation action when needed to protect the project.
